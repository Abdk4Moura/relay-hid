## Short description (≤80 chars)
Turn your phone into a keyboard, trackpad & remote for any device. No account.

## Full description
Relay turns your Android phone into a real keyboard, trackpad, and remote control for your
other devices — your iPad, Mac, Windows or Linux PC, smart TV, or console.

Two ways to connect:
• **Bluetooth** — your phone appears as a standard Bluetooth keyboard/mouse. Works with iPads,
  computers, TVs and consoles. No app needed on the other device.
• **Wi‑Fi (computers)** — run the free, open‑source Relay desktop receiver on your Mac, Windows
  or Linux machine for a richer link: clipboard sync, file drop, instant typing, and an absolute
  trackpad. Pair in one tap with a QR code.

Features:
• Full keyboard with every modifier (⌘ ⌥ ⌃ ⇧), arrows, function and media keys
• Adaptive layout — a one‑handed thumb keyboard in portrait, a full desktop keyboard in landscape
• Multi‑touch trackpad with tap‑to‑click, two‑finger scroll and gestures
• Type & Send — type or dictate on your phone, then fire it to the other device as keystrokes
  (great for logins, Wi‑Fi keys, long URLs and TV search boxes)
• Remote & presenter mode — D‑pad, media keys, slide controls
• Target profiles (iPad / Mac / Windows / Android TV / Apple TV / Linux / PlayStation) that map
  the right modifier keys automatically
• Clipboard sync and file drop to your computer over Wi‑Fi
• Reliable auto‑reconnect

Private by design: no account, no servers, no ads, no analytics. Everything stays on your own
devices and local network. See the privacy policy.
